By-year layout

Finally, a readable citation graph.

Most tools force-simulate node positions, which favours structure over meaning and collapses into an unreadable hairball as a graph grows.

Oignon lays every paper on its publication year instead. The seminal work swells where references converge; gaps in your reading show up as empty space.

And because oignon reads forward as well as back, you see not just a field's roots but the newest work extending it — coverage most tools stop short of.
