# Changelog

What's new in oignon. Most recent first.

## Unreleased

- **Shareable graphs.** A Share button on any graph creates a short
  `oignon.dev/g/<code>` link. Open it and the full graph loads, no signup, works
  for graphs of any size.
- **New home at oignon.dev.** The tool moved from GitHub Pages to its own domain,
  with a landing page at the root and the app at `oignon.dev/app`. Existing users
  on the old address get a one-click button to bring their saved library across.
- **Bibliography import.** Paste a `.bbl`/BibTeX file or a list of DOIs and build
  a single graph from many papers at once, to see what your reference list is
  adjacent to but missing.
- **Graph export.** Download the whole graph as JSON.
- Feedback and changelog links added in-app.
- Faster, more reliable metadata: joined the OpenAlex polite pool.
- Dark-mode fixes in the bibliography panel.

## 1.1.0

- Multi-paper graphs, graph export, and assorted fixes.
